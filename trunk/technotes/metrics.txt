Frame Extents (L,R,T,B)
	Requirements: A top level window object to retrieve extents for

Drag(X|Y) threshhold
	Requirements: Varies by screen on gtk

	Description:
	DRAG(X|Y) specifies the disance from the mouse click in the x and y directions that the mouse is allowed to move before starting a drag. DRAGX of 2 means that if the mouse moves 3 pixels to the left (or right) of the click position, dragging will begin, but not if it moved 2 pixels.
